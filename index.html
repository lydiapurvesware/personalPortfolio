About me

I am a, what some would call retired, professional dancer of 13 years. I have been dancing and training for 27 years now in Ballet, Contemporary, Modern, Jazz, and Hip-Hop. You may wonder what that has to do with programming and the answer is nothing and everything.

I have always had a fascination of computers and their laguages, logic, and abilities but as a dancer never really had time to devote to their exploration. Thanks(?) to the Covid-19 pandemic of 2020 I was able to revamp first, my priorities, then my desires, then my entire life and schedule. I (mostly) quit the dance industry and am now well on my way to becoming a programmer.

I love learning new things and am excited about the limitless possibilities that technology can offer and evolve to.
